# Signage Templates

Print these on weatherproof label stock or laminate them.

Attach to the TreeGuard frame at eye level.

## Files

- `signage-en.svg` — English
- (Translations welcome — see CONTRIBUTING.md)

## Content guidelines

Every sign must include:
1. **"PLAIN WATER ONLY"** — prominent, top of sign
2. **"1 bottle (~1L) maximum"** — prevents overwatering
3. **QR code** — links to tree info page
4. **TreeGuard logo/name** — for recognition and project awareness

Keep text minimal. The sign must be readable in 3 seconds while walking past.
